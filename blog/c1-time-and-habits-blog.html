Time and Habits

Below is a reflection on time and habit techniques

By Nick Shanks

2 July 2016


Which timebox and habits did you try?

I am giving the Pomodoro Techinque a go while doing this blog. I have always had issues with being easily distracted by outside sources while doing work. I am hoping this will keep me focussed on the task at hand.

What did you find interesting?

In the past I have used meditation, in particular moving meditation, while swimming. Diving deeper into the other realms of meditation looks interesting. I tried to not get caught in a rabbit hole while looking through this info but the concept of daily mindfulness meditation looks cool also.

What worked for you?

The Pomodoro Technique is working well so far! I still have 20 minutes left!!

Which will you experiment further with?

Pomodoro technique and Mindfullness Meditation stuck out to me the most but I am also keen to try out the Fogg Method.
